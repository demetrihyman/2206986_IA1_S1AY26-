Blog Review

AI-powered virtual assistants

How it can be applied in the world?
Everyone uses a virtual assistant, be it asking BixBy a question or using a personal finance app to create expense reports.
Why would it be useful?
It would be useful as it allows people to do various things from a basic question to helping with more complex issues.
Ethical issues around the idea?
  • Privacy and Surveillance
  • AI transparency
  • Human oversight and control
Provide moral solutions?
Privacy and Surveillance - The best solution is to let any user know the level of privacy and surveillance that they would be under.
AI transparency -Some solutions are to disclose Ai use clearly, never hiding automation,get informed consent before collecting or using personal data and open algorithms for independent audit(without exposing data).
Human oversight and control -A solution to this is to assign clear legal and ethical responsibility to a human operator for all AI actions.
Provide an example of an actual app in this area?
There are multiple apps like this. Some are Bixby, Cortana and Siri
